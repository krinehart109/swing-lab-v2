📷 Filming a good swing

A few seconds of setup makes the AI analysis far more accurate.
1
Film face-on

Put the camera directly in front of you (or directly down the target line), about 10–12 feet away — not at an angle.

2
Get your whole body in frame

Head to feet, with a little room above and below. The AI needs to see your shoulders, hips, and the club.

3
Steady the phone

Lean it against something or use a small tripod. A still camera lets the auto-trim and pose tracking lock on.

4
Good, even light

Face the light; avoid filming into the sun. Bright, plain backgrounds track best.

5
Keep it short

One swing per clip. Start a second or two before you take the club back and stop after the finish — the app auto-trims the rest.
